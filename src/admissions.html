Admissions 2026–27

Join our university community. Explore programs, eligibility, and how to apply.

✅ Applications Open: Admissions for Fall 2026–27 are now open. Last date to apply: June 30, 2026.

Undergraduate Programs

  • BS Computer Science (4 Years)
  • BS Business Administration (4 Years)
  • BS Electrical Engineering (4 Years)
  • BS Social Sciences (4 Years)
  • BS Mathematics (4 Years)
  • BS Biotechnology (4 Years)

Postgraduate Programs

  • MS Computer Science (2 Years)
  • MBA (2 Years)
  • MS Electrical Engineering (2 Years)
  • MS Data Science (2 Years)
  • PhD Computer Science (3–5 Years)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Undergraduate: Minimum 60% marks in Intermediate / A-Levels. Entry test score required.
  • MS Programs: 16 years of education (Bachelor's degree) with minimum 2.5 CGPA or 60% marks.
  • PhD Programs: MS degree with minimum 3.0 CGPA. Research proposal required.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Attested copy of Matriculation / O-Level certificate
  • Attested copy of Intermediate / A-Level certificate
  • Attested copy of Bachelor's degree (for MS applicants)
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Copy of CNIC / B-Form
  • Domicile certificate
  • Character certificate from last institution
