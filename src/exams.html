Examination Regulations

  1. Students must arrive at least 15 minutes before the examination begins.
  2. No student will be allowed to enter the hall after 15 minutes of the exam start time.
  3. Mobile phones, smart watches, and electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  4. Students must bring their University ID Card and Admit Card for each exam.
  5. Any form of cheating will result in immediate disqualification and disciplinary action.
  6. Students are not allowed to leave the examination hall during the first 30 minutes.
